Moreover, it looks for the possible extensions of the current QTL analysis methods to help on understanding regulatory mechanism at transcriptional and or molecular level.

Objective

  • Developing a generalized web database for visualization and analysis of omics-QTL mapping output allow verified users to upload QTL datasets via a generalized upload module
  • accompanied with an admin data management module to maintain data consistency and to ensure the atomicity of transactions
  • adapt the database model and visualization tools to accept QTL datasets of other species
  • expand the existing web portal towards other model species e.g. C.elegansand S.lycopersicum
  • optimization of platform performance
  • Dissection of gene regulatory network by means of genetical genomics interpret eQTL mapping output
  • identify co-expression co regulation (CECR) modules or clusters
  • Prioritization of candidate genes underlying eQTL based on functional gene network information
  • search motif(s) significantly associated with genes linked to one or more GO terms
